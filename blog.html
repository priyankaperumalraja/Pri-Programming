Welcome!

By: Priyanka (Pri) Perumalraja

Hello! Welcome to Pri-Programming and the Breakpoint Blog. My name is Priyanka and I’m the founder of these platforms. I created Pri-Programming and the Breakpoint Blog with the intention of creating an inclusive and accessible community where people can learn Computer Science, share their thoughts and experiences in this field, and learn about the thoughts and experiences of others. Many groups often feel excluded from the Computer Science field, myself included. I found it difficult to find communities that felt supportive and motivating, especially as a woman in a STEM field, and the feeling of ostracization nearly moved me to leave the field entirely. Computer Science is such a beautiful subject that embodies everything I love about science, innovation, and people’s capacity to create, so the fact that I was thinking of leaving because of this isolation absolutely crushed me. So, here we are! Pri-Programming is meant to provide Computer Science education resources that share CS concepts in interactive, easy-to-understand methods; while the Breakpoint Blog is meant to provide a space for people in the community to share their stories, either personally or anonymously, and learn from each other’s stories, hopefully highlighting the prejudices, concerns, and solutions to these issues. While this initiative is only just starting, I really hope that we can create a strong, supportive, and passionate community of diverse Computer Scientists! Thanks for listening, talk next time! (Also P.S., if you'd like to share an experience or thought of yours, feel free to send an inquiry to our contact email below and we’ll get back to you shortly!) - Pri :)
